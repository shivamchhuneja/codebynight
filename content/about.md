Hey, I’m Shivam.

I am a GTM engineer in AI security and governance. Outside work, I spend a lot of time learning backend systems, Linux, machine learning, and how the technical stuff I use every day actually works.

CodeByNight started because I wanted to learn programming properly instead of only working around tools that other people built. I was coming from marketing, websites, funnels, e-commerce, and growth work, so I already knew what real-world workflows looked like. I just wanted to understand more of the code, data, systems, and decisions under them.

This is where I write about that. Some posts are about GTM work and automation. Some are about AI security, Linux, backend learning, machine learning, or things I built and got stuck on for longer than I expected.

I mainly write to think through what I am learning and keep a record of it. If it helps someone else who is trying to get more technical without pretending they already know everything, that is great too.
